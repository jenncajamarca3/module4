Sunchoke Health Facts and Information

What are Sunchokes?

Sunchokes, also known as Jerusalem Artichokes, are a root vegetable native to North America. Despite the name, they are not related to artichokes and are actually a species of sunflower. They are high in **Inulin**, a prebiotic fiber.

Key Nutritional Benefits

  • **Inulin:** Supports a healthy gut microbiome and is good for digestion.
  • **Low Glycemic Index:** Digested slowly, which helps manage blood sugar levels.
  • **Vitamins and Minerals:** Rich in iron, potassium, and thiamine.
